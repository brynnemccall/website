About

Hello! My name is Brynne Winkelman and I am a sophomore New Media major with a photography minor at Molloy College. My main focus is Photography, but I am also skilled in graphic design and video editing as well. I am a media scholar in MolloyLife media, so I help make social media posts, take photos at on-campus events, and help out all departments wherever it is needed. I also run my photography business, @wink_photo on instagram, taking portait, family, and couples photos. For another college class, I created a blog called collelifeunraveled.com where I talk about my college experience and give advice to other current and incoming college students. Featured on this website is my work using Photoshop and Premiere in my Advanced Online Content Production, but if you would like to see more of my work please visit my other sites that I have listed. Thanks for visiting my website!
